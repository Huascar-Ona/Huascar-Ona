About

Founder and CEO, Andrew Jones, played a lot of golf growing up in the
state of Michigan but shortly after college he moved west to the Silicon
Valley where he seldom played due to that lack of a golf network and a
busy schedule. After working on a separate golf related startup, Andrew
decided he needed to get back to playing regularly but found it
challenging to find golfers with similar abilities that were available at a
convenient time and location to him. Additionally, he wanted to be able to
still play with buddies back home and track his own performance.

Golf Rival was created to provide golfers with a better platform to connect
and compete at a time and place that is convenient. Whether you are
looking to stay connected with friends living in another part of the globe
by competing in virtual events or being able to find local matches and
tournaments, we aim to provide our users with the best possible solution.
Additionally, our goal is to enable golfers to improve their own
performance by tracking & comparing statistics, providing live
tournament results, and giving fun incentives for improvement.
