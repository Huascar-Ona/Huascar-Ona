Tutorial: Create an Event

Now let’s start with the basics, choose the name of your event, the type of your event, and where you want your event to take place. You may want to hover over the for a quick description.

Name of the Event:
Type of the Event:
  • Single Game
    One round of 9 or 18 holes with a maximum of 8 players
  • Tournament
    Up to four rounds of play with cumulative scoring. Requires single scoring format and golf course.
  • Tournament Series
    Up to 15 rounds of play with both event winners and cumulative winner. Can be in different formats and at different courses for each round.
  • Local Event
    Players will attend in person on the date you specify
  • Virtual Event
    Players can play the course of their choosing by a date you specify
