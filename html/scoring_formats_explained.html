Scoring Formats Explained

Handicap Types :

Handicap Description
USGA Uses USGA handicap for all players as a basis
Callaway 1-day HCP system that can be used in events where most of the golfers do not have real handicap indexes
Manual Set all players handicaps manually
No Handicap Gross scores will be used

Scoring System :

Team or Individual Type Scoring Method Definition Handicap Allowance Default
Individual Stroke Medal Play Lowest amount of total strokes wins 100% Course HCP
Stableford Points values are assigned to the number strokes/hole and the highest score wins 100%Course HCP
Individual Match Std Match Match play is a hole-by-hole game where the lowest score wins the hole. 100% Course HCP
Team Stroke Medal Play Lowest amount of total team strokes wins 100% Course HCP
Stableford Points values are assigned to the number strokes/hole and the highest combined team score wins 100% Course HCP
Best Ball Each player on the team plays his or her own golf ball throughout the round, and on each hole the low score - or "best ball" - of the group serves as the team score. 80% Course HCP
Alternate Shot Two golfers play as a team and alternate hitting the same golf ball. 50% of the partners' combined Course HCP
Scramble In a scramble, each player tees off on each hole. The best of the tee shots is selected and all players play their 2nd shots from that spot. The best of the 2nd shots is determined, then all play their 3rd shots from that spot, and so on until the ball is holed. 100% Course HCP
Team Match Std Match Match play is a hole-by-hole game where the lowest combined score wins the hole. 100%Course HCP
Fourball Each player on the team plays his or her own golf ball throughout the round, and on each hole the low score - or "best ball" - of the group serves as the team score. 100% Course HCP
Foursomes Two golfers play as a team and alternate hitting the same golf ball. 50% of the partners' combined Course HCP
